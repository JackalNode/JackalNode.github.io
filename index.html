Something Different

Want an app made just for you?

StartGuard and PingGuard are free for everyone. This is different — I build you your own app, made exactly the way you need it.

Most of what I make is free for anyone to download. This is the one thing that isn't. I build a custom app just for you, or your business, made around exactly what you need — not a copy of something else with your name stuck on it. Once it's built, it's yours. You can use it, and if you want, you can even sell it to other people. There's a small cost either way — here's how that works.

What it costs
  • $1 a month keeps your app running. A bit like a phone contract — pay it, and the app works. It's not a payment for extra work, just the cost of keeping it switched on.
  • If you give or sell copies to other people, each copy also costs $1 a month. Say you build a little business selling the app to five people — that's $5 a month total, not $1. Every copy needs its own small payment to keep working, because keeping each one running costs me something too.
  • Building the app itself is a separate, one-off cost. We agree this together before I start any work, based on what you actually need. I try to keep this fair — lower for someone just starting out, more for a bigger, already-established business.

Why does it need a monthly payment at all? Every app I build checks in every so often to make sure it's still paid for — a bit like how Netflix checks your subscription is active before it'll play a show. Pay the $1, and it works normally. If a payment is missed, that one app pauses until it's sorted out. It doesn't collect your data or watch what you're doing — it just checks "has this been paid?" and nothing else.
